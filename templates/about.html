My Journey

From Starlight to Soul Light

My journey into the mystical arts began under the vast canopy of stars during childhood nights spent wondering about the cosmos and its infinite wisdom. What started as curiosity blossomed into a lifelong calling.

After years of studying Vedic astrology, tarot, and various spiritual traditions, I founded Celestial Readings — a sanctuary where ancient celestial wisdom meets modern understanding. Each reading I offer is infused with deep intuition, scholarly knowledge, and genuine care for every soul who seeks guidance.

I believe that the stars don't dictate our fate — they illuminate our choices. My mission is to help you see the paths available to you and empower you to choose with clarity, confidence, and cosmic awareness.
